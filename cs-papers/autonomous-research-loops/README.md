# Autonomous AI Research Loops

**A Self-Sustaining Architecture for Continuous Scientific Discovery**

*Hróar Þór Reynisson and Claude (Anthropic), with the Sefirot Autonomous Research System*

## Abstract

We present Keter, an architecture for autonomous AI research loops that enables continuous scientific investigation without human intervention. The system completed 100+ autonomous research cycles, accumulated 279 verified knowledge claims, closed 10 research arcs, and produced a submission-ready manuscript.
